About

Hi everyone, welcome to my site, Ventures in Yarn. If you are a beginner, experienced knitter or crafter, or would like to get started at knitting, this is the place for you! Here you will find my own knitting patterns, along with other crafting projects I have done.

I have been knitting since I was young, and really got into it during the Covid-19 lockdown, when I started crocheting as well. I hope you enjoy my patterns and please do get in touch with any questions you might have.
